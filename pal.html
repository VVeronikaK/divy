Palace of Culture and Science

It is really a showcase of Warsaw and the tallest building in Poland (it is 237 m high). The Palace of Culture and Science is the work of social realism. The building, which was built between 1952 and 1955, was a kind of "gift" of the Soviet Union for Poland.
